EU Small Online Businesses

This list is meant to create an overview of small businesses/online shops in the EU. With recent VAT changes it's become increasingly expensive to import goods from outside the EU. This list is meant to stimulate buying from small businesses within the EU and create a better overview of all the beautiful goods created on our little continent.

This website does not generate any profit through affiliate links or otherwise, it's just a database.
